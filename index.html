Welcoming Faith Organizations

Within many faith organizations and religious denominations, there are movements that support full inclusion of LGBT persons in the life of that organization or denomination. Most of these welcoming/reconciling/inclusive movements are not a part to the faith organization's or religious denomination's official structure, but they are working to change the policies of their organization or denomination. In addition, they provide support to LGBT persons within their churches.
